## Recommended Hotels

The conference has arranged options with three nearby hotels and one dormitory hotel for students. Please use the listed booking links or reservation contacts and include the conference code where noted.

### Grand Hotel International Prague
An iconic four-star hotel and national heritage site featuring unique socialist-realism architecture and grand interiors in a quiet part of Prague 6. The hotel is two tram stops away from the conference venue, or about a 15-minute walk.  

- **Website:** [grandhotel.cz](https://www.hotelint.cz/)
- **Booking link:** [Book hotel directly](https://www.hotelint.cz/en/booking-search/?checkin-date=2026-09-16&checkout-date=2026-09-18&adults-count=1&LanguageID=18&currency=EUR&promo-code=Conf_IMEKO2026#/rooms)
- **Promo code:** *Conf_IMEKO2026*

### Hotel Diplomat Prague
A premier four-star business hotel located directly at the Dejvická metro station, offering modern amenities and spectacular views of Prague Castle. The hotel is situated in the immediate vicinity of the campus, only a 5-minute walk from the conference venue.

- **Website:** [hoteldiplomat.cz](https://www.hoteldiplomat.cz/)
- Please book through the hotel reservation system and include code *IMEKO2026* in your reservation.
- The hotel does not hold a dedicated room block for this event.

### Hotel DAP (Volareza)
A modern three-star hotel located directly on Vítězné náměstí, offering a convenient central location in Dejvice with excellent access to public transport and local restaurants. The hotel is situated just a 5-minute walk from the conference venue.
